# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

The community commits to creating an inclusive environment that respects the dignity and contributions of all people, regardless of identity characteristics such as race, ethnicity, disability, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, socioeconomic status, or education level.

## Encouraged Behaviors

Community members strive to:

1. Respect the purpose and gatherings of the community
2. Engage kindly and honestly with others
3. Honor different viewpoints and experiences
4. Take responsibility for actions and contributions
5. Accept constructive feedback gracefully
6. Commit to repairing harm when needed
7. Promote community well-being through positive conduct

## Restricted Behaviors

Prohibited actions include:

1. Harassment—violating expressed boundaries or unwanted personal attention
2. Character attacks—insulting or demeaning comments toward members
3. Stereotyping or discrimination based on immutable identities
4. Inappropriate sexualization in community contexts
5. Sharing private information without permission
6. Violence or threats toward persons or groups
7. Threats to community well-being

### Additional Restrictions

- Impersonation or identity deception
- Failing to credit content sources
- Inappropriate promotional or commercial content
- Irresponsibly presenting restricted behaviors

## Reporting and Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:

- Opening an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/adptly/purelymail-enhancer/issues)
- Contacting the project maintainers directly

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Moderators are committed to maintaining confidentiality regarding the reporter of an incident and will provide transparency where possible.

### Enforcement Guidelines

Community moderators will follow these enforcement guidelines when determining consequences for violations:

1. **Warning** - A private, written warning for minor first-time violations
2. **Temporary Limited Activity** - A temporary restriction from participating in certain community spaces
3. **Temporary Suspension** - A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community
4. **Permanent Ban** - A permanent ban from any sort of public interaction within the community

The enforcement action taken will be based on the severity and frequency of the violation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all community spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code reviews
- Project documentation
- Any other project-related communication

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
